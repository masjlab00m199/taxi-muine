Top Attractions in Mui Ne

Popular places for travelers visiting Mui Ne for the first time.

🏜️

White Sand Dunes

One of the most famous attractions in Mui Ne. Visitors often come here for sunrise, wide sand views, and ATV experiences.

🌅

Red Sand Dunes

Known for warm red-orange sand and beautiful sunset scenery. It is a popular stop for quick sightseeing and photos.

🌊

Fairy Stream

A gentle stream with unique natural formations. Many visitors enjoy walking barefoot through the shallow water.

🎣

Mui Ne Fishing Village

A great place to experience local fishing life, see colorful boats, and enjoy the coastal atmosphere in the morning.

🏛️

Po Sah Inu Cham Towers

A historical Cham site that adds cultural value to a Mui Ne trip, beyond beaches and dunes.

🏖️

Mui Ne Beach Area

Perfect for relaxing, enjoying sea views, and spending quiet time near resorts and restaurants.
